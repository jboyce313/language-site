Travelers Language Assistance

What do you need help with?

Travelers' Translation Unit

Travelers' Translation Unit is currently dedicated to handling Spanish - English translation communication between Claim Professionals and Insureds, Claimants or Injured Workers (refer to link section for additional program information). Claim Professionals can access a bilingual Travelers professional for inbound or outbound translations needed. Translators are trained in inurance fundamentals.

Process:

1. Claim Professional dials 866-678-0495 - the customer can be on the line, or the Translator can initiate the call to the customer.

2. Translator will set expectations with Claim Professional and customer before translation begins to ensure a seamless conversation.

3. Use Skype for private communication between Translator and Claim Professional.

Hours of Operation: 8 am to 8 pm ET, Monday to Friday - Translators are based in Orlando and Houston.

Note: Automatic transfer to Language Line if Translators are unavailable.

Claim Translation Volunteers

To request language assistance from a Travelers volunteer:

1. Click the language you need assistance with from the list below. An IM or e-mail will automatically generate to volunteers who can assist with that language.

2. Describe your request and a language resource, if available, will respond.

3. Record your request using the New Language Request form.

Language Line

Process:

1. Place the Limited English Speaker on hold

2. Dial 1-800-874-9426

3. Enter on your telephone keypad or provide the representative with the 6-digit client ID - 709509

4. Press 1 for Spanish or Press 2 for all other languages. Speak the name of the langauge at the prompt. You may press or say 0 to stayon the line for assistance

5. Personal code: your AFO number

6. Supply claim number to the representative when prompted

7. Brief the interpreter

8. Add the Limited English speaker to the line
